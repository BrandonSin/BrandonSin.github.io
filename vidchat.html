Video Chat for Toddlers


An Vibrant Interactive Take on Chat App


The goal of this Project was to create a chat application that is applicable for a toddler. Specifically an application that can be utilized for young grandchildren and grandparents. With that in mind, I wanted to do something that is vibrant and catchy to keep toddlers attracted to the application. In the final product, I have decided to go with a sound board coupled with animation.






Design Choices

  • Colorful panels create attraction
  • Cartoony Characters and fast moving animation keeps children attentive
  • Positioning of Video Feeds are to provide more interaction with the panels while having the ability to see each other


Since this is an application for toddlers, there's a lot of room for creativity. This ranges from virtual face painting , music game, "shake the bottle" to "peekaboo". These are some of the rough drafts for the app.



I have decided to go with a sound board that plays Animation. This application can be educational such as learning different types of coins/shapes/colors otherwise it can be used as a fun application full sound and animation.
